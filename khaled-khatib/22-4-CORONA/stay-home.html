What should I do if I got infected or got symptoms
Stay Home. Governor Gary R. Herbert has issued a “Stay Safe, Stay Home” directive to all Utahns. This is not a shelter in place order, but rather the next step in the Urgent Phase laid out in the Utah Leads Together plan. This directive seeks to make clear what individuals and businesses should do to slow the spread of COVID-19 in our state and communities. Goals of the directive are to flatten the curve, reduce the strain on hospitals and the healthcare system, and minimize the impact on medical resources for those with highest need. The governor’s original executive order was set to expire on April 13, but the order has been extended through May 1, 2020. You can read the updated directive here.
